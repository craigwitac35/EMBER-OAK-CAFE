Welcome

A neighborhood spot built to feel familiar

Read Our Story

Local, warm, and steady

Ember & Oak is a simple kind of place. Good coffee, honest food, and a room full of people who keep coming back. We roast with care, bake daily, and keep the vibe calm.

Whether you are grabbing a quick espresso or settling in with a book, you have a seat here.
